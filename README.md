# 🥗 UPF – Smart Food Awareness App

UPF is a mobile application designed to help people make better food choices by understanding what they eat.  
The app focuses on identifying **ultra-processed foods (UPF)**, chemical additives, and their potential long-term health effects in a simple and accessible way.

## 📱 App Overview

Many people choose food based on convenience without realizing the long-term health consequences.  
UPF helps users become more aware of food ingredients and processing levels, enabling healthier decisions without disrupting daily routines.

**One-sentence pitch:**  
> *An app that helps people understand what is really inside their food and avoid harmful ultra-processed products.*

---

## 🎯 Target Users

- **Age:** 15–90 years old  
- **Occupation:** Any profession that prioritizes health  
- **Location:** Urban and suburban areas in Thailand  
- **Tech Skill Level:** Normal  
- **Main Problem:** Choosing easy and convenient meals without considering future health impacts  

---

## 🚀 Core Features (MVP)

- 🔍 **Food & Product Scanner**  
  Scan barcodes or take photos of food products to view ingredient information.

- 🧪 **Ingredient & Chemical Breakdown**  
  Displays nutritional values, additives, and chemical components in simple language.

- ⚠️ **Health Risk Summary**  
  Shows potential long-term health risks from frequent consumption.

- 🔁 **Healthy Alternatives**  
  Suggests better and more convenient food options.

- 👤 **Profile & Scan History**  
  Stores personal information and records past scans.
